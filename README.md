# AI SEO Blog & Content Cluster Generator  
### Case Study: Digital Marketing Agency (Bangalore)

---

## Overview
This project is an AI-powered SEO content generation system designed to create high-quality, search-optimized blog content for business websites.

It generates:
- Pillar blogs (long-form SEO articles)  
- Supporting blogs (content clusters)  
- SEO-optimized structures (H1–H3)  
- Internal linking strategies  
- Local SEO-focused content  

The goal is to replicate how professional SEO teams plan and produce ranking content.

---

## Problem
Most business websites fail to rank on search engines because:
- Content lacks proper structure  
- Search intent is not clearly targeted  
- No content cluster or internal linking strategy  
- Poor local SEO optimization  

This results in low visibility, traffic, and conversions.

---

## Solution
This project uses a structured AI prompt system to generate complete SEO blog packs that:
- Target specific keywords and search intent  
- Follow proven SEO structures  
- Build topical authority through content clusters  
- Optimize content for local search (Bangalore-based queries)  

---

## Business Context
**Business Type:** Digital Marketing Agency  
**Services:** Local SEO & Performance Marketing  
**Target Audience:** Small business owners  
**Location:** Bangalore  

---

## Prompting Strategies Used

### 1. Role-Based Prompting
The AI is assigned the role of an SEO Content Strategist and Blog Writer.  
This ensures outputs are strategic, keyword-focused, and aligned with real SEO practices.

---

### 2. Structured Prompting
The prompt is divided into clear sections:
- Role  
- Business details  
- Task  
- Search intent  
- Content cluster strategy  
- Output format  

This ensures consistency, clarity, and reusability across different use cases.

---

### 3. Search Intent Targeting
Content is generated based on **informational and transactional intent**, ensuring it:
- Matches what users are searching for  
- Provides helpful answers  
- Encourages business action  

---

### 4. Content Cluster Strategy
The system generates:
- One pillar blog (broad topic)  
- 3–5 supporting blogs (long-tail topics)  

All blogs are interconnected through internal linking, improving SEO authority and site structure.

---

### 5. Local SEO Optimization
Content is optimized for local search by:
- Including location-based keywords (Bangalore)  
- Targeting service + location queries  
- Making content relevant for local users  

---

## How It Works
1. Input business details and target audience  
2. Define keyword intent and topic focus  
3. AI generates:
   - Pillar blog  
   - Supporting blogs  
   - Internal linking plan  
   - SEO metadata  

The output is ready to be used on a business website.
